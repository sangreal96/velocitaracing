Our Sponsors

Pretty Patio is one of Bangalore’s sought after Landscape Company. With a main focus on upcyling, Pretty Patio offers a wide range of products and solutions to both homes and workspaces. Pretty Patio also undertakes garden makeovers and has done up some of Bangalore’s famous watering holes and restaurants. Bringing colour and lush green back to our city, Pretty Patio is a one stop solution to all your gardening/landscaping needs! The company has been constantly supporting us since our inception in 2015 and we couldn’t have been prouder to have them on board.

Established by a group of farmers in 1931, Mangaluru, Vijaya Bank is one of India’s prominent nationalized banks today. Headquartered in Bangalore presently, Vijaya Bank reported a business of Rs.2,29,000 crore in March 2017.Vijaya Bank offers a bouquet of innovative and attractive products and services to its customers. The Bank offers several technology products, such as, ATMs, Cash Deposit Machines, Debit and Credit cards, internet banking, Mobile Banking, Tab Banking, e-Passbook, Mobile Wallet, Funds transfer through RTGS and NEFT etc. The Bank also offers RuPay cards to its customers. Velocita Racing is proudly sponsored by the MSRIT branch of Vijaya Bank.

Without its nuts and bolts to hold it together, a vehicle is nothing but metal, rubber and grease. Smartlink Inc. is another company that has partnered with us since 2015 and provides us with the best fasteners in town. Without compromising on quality, Smartlink Inc. has a wide range of fasteners to suite your every need. Year after year, our partnership has grown stronger and we intend to make it last forever. We are just overwhelmed having Smartlink Inc. on board with us.

Microtex is a leading technology company that manufactures Industrial Batteries for storage of power, in Bangalore, India. The factory has a covered area of 26700 Sq ft on 5 acres of land. Established in 1969, 49 years ago it is a company well known for its high quality products. Microtex produces in-house,  specially designed lead alloys, lead oxides, grid castings, pasted plates, injection molded containers, multi-tubular gauntlets, PVC separators and produces the complete battery using state of the art industry standard battery making machinery. Leading India in developing batteries conforming to International standards, Velocita Racing is proud to have a pioneer such as Microtex Energy Pvt. Ltd. sponsor us.

Started back in 2000, Arjuna Granites has been providing excellent products and service in terms of natural stone. Procuring natural stones of top quality from select quarries across the country, the company’s name is associated with quality in the Indian Granite Industry. Any type of Indian Granite material procured from top quarries is processed at their factory thus supplying high quality natural stones. This strategy has helped the company in registering a steep growth in both volume and its varieties. Arjuna Granites helped us relentlessly during the fabrication of VR 2.0 and we are indebted towards them. We feel lucky to have them on board as our sponsor.
