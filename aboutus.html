About Us

We at Velocita, are a team of undergraduate students from Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bangalore, who are continuously working on the development of racing grade vehicles for various competitions across India, while simultaneously working on mechanical field projects.

Velocita Racing came to life in 2015 around a small table at the MSRIT canteen, where a group of students from Mechanical and Industrial Management found themselves in the midst of fellow automobile enthusiasts. In our initial enthusiasm we began preparations for the SAE Supra in full swing but soon realized that as first year students, the task was too great to execute. Humbled by the realization, we decided to make our debut at the 3rd Season of NKRC in 2015. Since then, weâ€™ve come a long way.
