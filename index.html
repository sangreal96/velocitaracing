About Us

We at Velocita, are a team of undergraduate students from Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bangalore, who are continuously working on the development of racing grade vehicles for various competitions across India, while simultaneously working on mechanical field projects.

CARS

VR1

Genesis. The first-born go-kart of Velocita Racing. Made in 2015, this 125cc shifter kart was built like a tank, and could definitely take a hit. It was built by the (then) all-first-year team, who started Velocita. Powered by a 5-speed, 125cc engine from the Bajaj Discover, the VR1 did have its flaws.

S.A.E

Society of Automotive Engineers

Society Of Automotive Engineers or SAE is an international organization which is based on the different aspects of automobile and aeronautical engineering. It is an organization into which students can register and partake in the different competitions held by the organization.

A few are Formula Supra and Baja, the aeronautical competitions are held in USA. The organization also organizes technical workshops and other events for it's members.

Most of the students in Velocita are part of this organization, presently we are looking forward to take part in the Formula style race car competitions held by the organization.
